[OBJECTIF]
Trouver des chaines YouTube actives (upload < 30 jours) de createurs 
bases a {city}, {state} dans la categorie {cat_label}, 
avec {sub_min} a {sub_max} abonnes.

[ETAPE 1 — Recherche directe]
# Chercher directement des listes de YouTubeurs locaux
Requetes a formuler:
- youtubers from {city} {cat_label}
- {city} {state} youtube creator {cat_label}
- best {cat_label} youtubers {city} OR {state}
- site:reddit.com {city} youtuber {cat_label} recommendation

[ETAPE 2 — Approche universitaire et institutionnelle]
# Identifier les programmes d'etude et diplomes pertinents
Requetes a formuler:
- {city} university {category} program graduates alumni youtube
- {state} film school OR cinema school students youtube channel
- {city} {cat_label} workshop instructor youtube

[ETAPE 3 — Communaute locale et evenements]
# Explorer les communautes locales et evenements
Requetes a formuler:
- {city} {cat_label} meetup convention festival creators
- {city} {cat_label} community youtube group
- {city} content creator collective network {category}

[ETAPE 4 — Presse locale et interviews]
# Chercher dans la presse locale des profils de createurs
Requetes a formuler:
- {city} local youtuber interview profile {cat_label}
- {city} rising creator local creator {cat_label}
- {city} newspaper magazine {cat_label} youtube creator

[ETAPE 5 — Reseaux sociaux croises]
# Trouver via Twitter/Instagram/TikTok vers YouTube
Requetes a formuler:
- site:twitter.com {city} youtube {cat_label} creator
- {city} {cat_label} tiktoker also on youtube
- {city} {cat_label} influencer subscribe new video

[ETAPE 6 — Recherche YouTube directe]
# Chercher directement des chaines YouTube via Google
Requetes a formuler:
- site:youtube.com {city} {cat_label}
- site:youtube.com {city} {state} {cat_label} subscribers
- inurl:youtube.com {city} {category} channel

[NOTES]
# Pour chaque etape, le programme:
# 1. Formule les requetes adaptees a la ville/categorie
# 2. Alterne entre Google et Brave Search
# 3. Navigue comme un humain (Firefox headless)
# 4. Sauvegarde chaque page de resultats en HTML
# 5. Parse les liens + descriptions
# 6. Le LLM analyse pour extraire noms, URLs YouTube, evidence ville
